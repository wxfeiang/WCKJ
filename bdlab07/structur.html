Each cell is unique and has its own complex structure. We have developed a disruptive technology that forthe first time allows users to explore living interiors in 3D without labels or other intrusive methods. 3D Cell Explorer is a high-speed, high-resolution, non-invasive tool that can explore deep into the biological system. This allows us to record amazing 3-D images of the entire cell in seconds, and to use a nanometer resolution rate higher than any common microscope on the market. Lysosomes, subcellular structures andnuclei of cells can be seen directly.
